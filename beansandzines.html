BRIEF

You will receive a Design Fiction describing an industry or service that needs a mobile first website and accompanying designed pieces that include but aren’t limited to: Identity systems, posters, menus, physical branding, advertisements, packaging, billboard, bus shelter designs etc. You will use existing images to mock up your designed pieces and display these within a multi-page InDesign template provided to you on blackboard and for you to re-design and present your work. More information will be detailed within the Design Fiction brief.

Website

You will be using the Bootstrap framework to create a mobile first multi-page website for a fictional client who requires an information heavy website. More information will be detailed within the Design Fiction provided to you in class.

Designed Pieces

You will be designing a logotype / identity, physical branding of an existing object (see design fiction),+ a selection of designed pieces chosen by you, listed within the design fiction provided. These designed pieces will be placed in a “mock up” style to show them within a fictional environment on display.

STRATEGY

My plan for my coffee and magazine mobile truck business is centered around providing a unique and convenient experience for customers, aged between 16-50, seeking a combination of quality coffee and unique and artistic magazines. The magazine aspect of the business will focus on carefully selecting a variety of publications spanning different genres and interests, ensuring there is something for everyone. However its main aim is to apeal to the creatives in the world but aslso show non creatives how interesting and fun it can be. I plan to establish strategic partnerships with local coffee roasters and magazine distributors to maintain a fresh and ever-evolving inventory. By carefully selecting prime locations and leveraging social media and online platforms, I aim to create a strong brand presence and build a loyal customer base. Through an inviting atmosphere within the mobile truck, I seek to provide a welcoming environment for customers to relax, indulge in a great cup of coffee, and discover captivating stories within the pages of their favorite magazines.

SCOPE

The scope of my coffee and magazine mobile truck business is to reach a wide range of customers in various locations and events, offering them a unique and enjoyable experience. By operating as a mobile truck, I have the flexibility to explore different neighborhoods, business districts, parks, festivals, and other popular gathering spots. This mobility allows me to tap into different customer demographics, expanding my reach and maximizing potential sales opportunities. Additionally, I plan to collaborate with local magazines, community organizations, and event organizers to set up pop-up locations. I will need to do this by seting up a booking section on my website.Therefore adding the possibility of partnering with corporate clients to provide on-site coffee and magazine services for their employees, conferences, or special events. I will also add features to the site such as telling us about magazines we may not own so that we can hopefully import them in and have a more fine tuned set of customers. I will also add a feature to be able to order your coffee through our website
